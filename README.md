# Customer Spending Behavior Analysis for Personalization of Banking Product Offerings
End-to-end Power BI project analyzing customer transaction data to assess financial health, identify spending behavior, and generate personalized banking product recommendations.

## Business Problem
How can banks leverage customer transaction data to identify spending behavior, assess financial health, and optimize personalized product offerings?

### My role
Transform Data → Define Metrics → Develop Analytical Questions → Visualize Dashboard → Insight

## Metrics & Dimensions
- Total Expense
- Total Expense (Operating)
- Total Income
- Net Cash Flow
- Avg Monthly Expense
- Expense Ratio
- Year Month
### Note:
Using DAX to create metrics and dimensions. Ignoring Credit Card Payment values, which are not consumption expenses.

## Analytical Question
- What is the customer's monthly cash flow?
- Which spending categories contribute the most to cash flow reduction?
- Which credit card is the most overspending?

### Insight
1. The customer demonstrates overall financial stability with a healthy expense-to-income ratio and consistent positive net cash flow, indicating low short-term credit risk.

2. The predominance of fixed expenses indicates predictable cash outflows, suitable for installment plans and refinancing.

3. Credit card usage is concentrated in optional lifestyle categories, indicating strong engagement potential for category-based rewards programs.

4. Temporary cash flow volatility indicates potential demand for short-term liquidity solutions.

5. The use of structured accounts reflects disciplined financial behavior, reducing exposure to credit risk.

### Recomendation
1. Optimize your credit card limit and offer additional wealth products (deposits, mutual funds) to customers.

2. Offer a variety of installment-based products.

3. Provide category-based rewards and personalized merchant promotions through app notifications, and offer 0% interest on high-value purchases.

4. Offer short-term revolving credit to provide customers with a loan limit they can use whenever they need it.

5. Offer auto-debit features and credit card tier upgrades with enhanced benefits.
